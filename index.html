How it works

One new ingredient, traced through the whole kitchen.

The Compliance Risk Graph is the engine underneath every feature: instead of storing documents in folders, it stores them as connected nodes linked to the food-safety obligation they belong to.

Step 1
Invoice arrives

New "chocolate sauce" detected on a supplier delivery note via OCR.

Step 2
Allergen register checked

Graph traversal looks for a matching allergen register entry.

Step 3
Menu & label checked

Menu item and customer-facing allergen declaration are checked too.

Step 4
Compliance gap

No register entry found, so the gap is flagged before the ingredient reaches the plate.

Step 5
Manager alerted

Single consolidated corrective task sent, before customer exposure.

Step 6
Score updates

Live Inspection Readiness Score adjusts in real time until it's resolved.

System architecture

Six layers, one connected compliance network.

Document intelligence, graph-based data modelling and rules-based compliance logic, working together. No machine learning is required to make the MVP work, that part comes later, trained on real operational data.

01
Data Capture

Mobile daily checks, document uploads, rota entries and corrective-action photos.

REST API · React Native · React.js
02
Document Intelligence

OCR + NLP extract structured data from invoices, certificates and allergen sheets.

Google Document AI / AWS Textract · spaCy
03
Operational Knowledge Base

Master records for staff, suppliers, ingredients, allergens, recipes and equipment.

PostgreSQL
04
Compliance Risk Graph, core innovation

Records become connected nodes linked to risks, evidence and corrective actions.

Neo4j / Apache AGE · custom graph traversal
05
Scoring & Alert Engine

Live Inspection Readiness Score, pre-shift alerts, allergen-gap and consistency alerts.

Python rules engine · scikit-learn / TensorFlow post-MVP · FastAPI
06
Reporting & Workflow

Dashboards, corrective-action lists, inspection-ready evidence packs, consultant views.

React.js · Puppeteer / WeasyPrint · RBAC
Core innovation

Five proprietary elements no competitor combines.

A novelty search across patent databases found no single patent, or combination of patents, revealing this architecture.

Innovation A

Compliance Risk Graph

Connects every operational record to the specific food-safety obligation it belongs to, rather than storing it as an isolated file.

Innovation B

Invoice-to-Allergen Gap Detection

Reads supplier invoices and delivery notes and checks new or changed ingredients against the allergen register before they reach the kitchen.

Innovation C

Rota-to-Training Risk Matching

Validates every shift assignment against the staff member's current training and certification status before the shift begins.

Innovation D

Live Inspection Readiness Score

A continually refreshed 0 to 100 compliance health score based on real operational evidence, not just ticked boxes.

Innovation E

Cross-Document Ingredient Intelligence

Reconciles invoices, recipes, menus and allergen files simultaneously to surface three-way mismatches automatically.

Innovation F

Unified Evidence Repository

A single ordered, traceable evidence store, so an EHO request for 30 days of records takes minutes, not a folder search.
